About

Built by people who've done the work.

Waystead is a small, independent studio run by two people whose backgrounds complement each other: over twenty years in engineering — designing and delivering large-scale network and telecoms systems — and a decade in education, tutoring and running a learning centre. Along the way we've started businesses of our own, and mentored others starting theirs.

We're taking that experience — engineering rigour, teaching craft, and the hard-won lessons of building from scratch — and turning it into tools that help people reach their own goals. Each product stands on its own, yet all share one aim: to make a demanding thing clearer, and keep you moving toward what matters. And we're only getting started — building a growing family of solutions to help more people, in more ways.
